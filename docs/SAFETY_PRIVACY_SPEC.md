# Safety And Privacy Spec

## MVP Constraints

The current StyleAI MVP is local and mocked.

Required constraints:

- No Android `INTERNET` permission.
- No real AI calls.
- No Gemini calls for product logic.
- No network calls.
- No real billing.
- No accounts.
- No cloud sync.
- No free-text prompts.
- No unrestricted image generation.
- No 3D or AR try-on.

## Style Profile

The style profile is optional.

Shopping checks must remain usable without:

- face upload;
- body upload;
- mandatory photo upload.

## Visual References

Looks visualizations are style references only.

Required disclaimer:

- EN: "Visualizations are style references, not exact clothing fit simulations."
- RU: "Визуализации показывают стилевое направление, а не точную посадку одежды."

## Prohibited Content

Do not add flows or assets involving:

- underwear;
- lingerie;
- swimwear;
- erotic content;
- nudity;
- sexualized styling;
- unsafe categories;
- competitor screenshots;
- copyrighted assets.

## Billing

Billing is mocked only. Credits are local UI state and must not trigger real purchases.
